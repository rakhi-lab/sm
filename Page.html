School Management - Education & Learning Management

School Management is a WordPress plugin to manage one or multiple schools and their entities such as classes, sections, students, exams, ID cards, admit cards, teachers, staff, fees, invoices, income, expense, noticeboard, study materials, staff/teacher can send the homework SMS on student/parents mobile number and much more.

Your school can take live classes of your students using Zoom, creating unlimited live sessions with our system. You can publish results, admit cards, and certificates publicly. Without any login, students can get results using their details.

Now eLearning management: You can upload subject-wise Video Classes (Recorded Video Classes) and Documents (PNG, JPG, PDF Files) or YouTube Videos.

Payment gateway: Paypal, Stripe, Pesapal, Paystack, Razorpay, PayTM, Bank Transfer, UPI Transfer.

SMS Service Providers: Nexmo, Twilio, MsgClub, SMS Striker, Msg91, TextLocal, EBulkSMS, Infigo SMS.

Android Mobile App: Allows students and parents to log in and manage student-related activities like homework, study material, book issue, class timetable, attendance, student profile details, exam results, admit cards, notifications, events, fee invoices, payment history, and more.

  • Teacher

  • Accountant
    Handles fee records, payments, and school budgeting.
  • Receptionist
